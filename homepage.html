TEAM NAME

The team name that our team has chosen is Memeiacs. The origin of our name came from the prefix “meme” that formally meant “an element of culture or system of behavior that is considered to be passed down from one individual to another”. Since the beginning of the 21st century, the word has adopted a new meaning to be “a humorous image, video, piece of text, etc. that is copied and spread rapidly by internet users”. (Oxford Dictionary n.d.) Team members at Memeiacs are very up-to-date with newest memes and are always eager to lighten up stressful working hours for others. We are aware it will come to the attention of potential employers and we hope they can see our goofy, bright personality through the name.
